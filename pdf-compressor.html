How to Compress PDF Files Online

Our free PDF compression tool makes it easy to reduce the file size of your PDF documents. Whether you need to send a PDF via email, upload it to a website, or just save storage space, our tool can help. The process is simple:

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping it into the box above or clicking to select a file
  2. Choose your compression settings (or use our recommended defaults)
  3. Click "Compress PDF" to start the process
  4. Download your compressed file when processing is complete

Why Compress PDF Files?

PDF compression is useful for many reasons:

  • Email attachments: Many email services have file size limits for attachments
  • Web uploads: Websites often restrict upload sizes for forms and applications
  • Storage savings: Reduce the space needed for your document archives
  • Faster sharing: Smaller files transfer more quickly over the internet
